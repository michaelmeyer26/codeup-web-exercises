Right in the heart of downtown Dallas, you can find our Elm Street location next door to The Joule Hotel’s garden, which features a
stunning, three-story Giant Eyeball. Join us for lunch or dinner on our shady patio or in one of our three dining rooms, we’re serving up
delicious homemade Italian food and pizza every day of the week.

You can find our Downtown Dallas Italian Restaurant right in the heart of the Main Street District. More specifically, this Campisi’s
resides in the Stone Street Gardens, a red brick pedestrian path between Elm Street and Main Street. This little urban oasis has great
views of the architecture downtown, a sculpture garden, and plenty of restaurants to choose from. Of course, we hope you choose to
visit Campisi’s! Stop by for Happy Hour Monday through Friday and enjoy $5 pizzas and cocktails, $3 beers, and plenty of other
discounted prices on many of our popular menu items, like toasted ravioli and calamari. If Happy Hour isn’t your thing, join us for Half
Price Wine Nights every Monday and Tuesday. Get 50% off bottles of wine with your homemade pastas and pizzas. Also, uniformed
Dallas police officers, firefighters, and paramedics receive 50% off their meals anytime.
